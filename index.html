About Ithuvia

The professor who tells you what others will not.

AI is transforming university life faster than most institutions can respond. While committees debate policy, students are already using ChatGPT, Claude, and other tools—often with no guidance and a great deal of risk.

Ithuvia is a student-first project led by a real professor. It translates “inside the classroom” experience into honest, practical systems you can use to:

  • plan and write stronger assignments with AI as a critical thinking partner;
  • avoid plagiarism traps and poorly designed assessment tasks;
  • use AI to design portfolios, side-hustles, and career assets that stand out.

Less ivory-tower theory. More real-world survival and success in an AI-powered university.
