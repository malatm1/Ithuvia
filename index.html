About Ithuvia

A social platform for the human side of AI in Higher Education.

AI is transforming university life faster than most institutions can respond. While committees debate policy, students are already using ChatGPT, Claude, and other tools—often with no guidance and a great deal of risk.

Ithuvia is being built as a student-only social platform where you can swap AI prompts, compare “what really works” in your modules, and learn from a professor who shows you how the academic system actually thinks.

Before the app launches, this site gives you early access to:

  • AI Study Systems: workflows to plan, draft, and “stress-test” your assignments with AI as a critical thinking partner.
  • Side-Hustle Studio: templates for using AI to design, test, and launch small projects and online services while you study.
  • Career Launchpad: guidance on building portfolios, CVs, and real evidence of skills that matter in a post-AI job market.

The goal is simple: help you move from confused AI user to confident architect of your own academic and career path.
